About CBL

Chendamangalam Badminton League — more than a tournament. It's a community, a passion, and a stage for every player.

Our Story

CBL was born from a simple love for badminton in Chendamangalam. What started as friends meeting for weekend games grew into a full-fledged league where teams compete, friendships strengthen, and the spirit of sport shines. We believe every smash, every drop, and every rally brings us closer — not just to the net, but to each other.

CBL stands for Chendamangalam Badminton League — a name that proudly carries our place and our game. It's the perfect blend of local pride and competitive badminton.

Our Mission

We aim to promote badminton at the grassroots level, give budding players a platform to showcase their talent, and build a healthy, active community. Through structured leagues, fair play, and a focus on teamwork, we want CBL to be the tournament that every badminton enthusiast in the region looks forward to.

What We Offer

  • League format — Round-robin matches leading to playoffs and finals.
  • Three competitive teams — Thunder Boys, Smash Masters, and Smart Boys — each with its own identity and fan base.
  • Transparent points table — Updated standings so everyone knows where they stand.
  • Fair play & sportsmanship — Respect for opponents, referees, and the game.
  • Community events — Beyond the court, we come together for gatherings and celebrations.

Why Badminton?

Badminton is fast, tactical, and accessible. It doesn't ask for a huge field or expensive gear — just a court, a racket, a shuttle, and the will to play. At CBL, we celebrate this accessibility and turn it into high-energy competition. Whether you're here to win or to enjoy the game, you're in the right place.

Join us. Cheer for your team. Feel the thrill of the CBL.
